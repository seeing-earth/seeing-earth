Global Situation Room

Understand the most urgent environmental challenges

How to Use

Hover or click on an environmental challenge to view details and explore major causes.

Then, click on causes to see more information, and explore local causes underneath.

This is an MVP for demonstration purposes.

Project Vision

Help people intuitively see the challenges affecting our world.

Enable people to model our world, across time and different possibilities, to tinker with possible futures and learn about the future they want to live in -- as well as what it takes to get there.
